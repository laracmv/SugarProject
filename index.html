How this is calculated: When all attendees leave simultaneously, vehicles queue at exit points causing 20-30 min of idle time per car. Staggering departures spreads this load. We estimate 60% of attendees arrive by private vehicle (1.8 occupants/car avg). Each staggered attendee reduces their vehicle's idle time by ~70% of their wait duration. CO2 savings use the EPA figure of 0.03 kg CO2 per minute of idling per passenger vehicle. Tree equivalence uses 21.77 kg CO2 absorbed per tree per year (European Environment Agency). Congestion time saved = (vehicles) x (idle minutes avoided). Avg exit time = baseline 25 min minus the proportional reduction from staggering.
